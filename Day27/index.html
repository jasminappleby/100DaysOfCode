Pokémon: Legends Arcues

Pokémon Legends: Arceus is an action role-playing game developed by Game Freak and published by Nintendo and The Pokémon Company for the Nintendo Switch. This game released on the 28th January 2022. It has a 7/10 rating on IGN, and a 84% rating on Metacritic.

Biggest Shock!

One of my favourite things about this game is the overexpressions which the characters have in cutscenes. They can make for such good meme images. Regardless of the meme quality these have, it also just makes the game feel less flat, and gives the characters personality.
